The Story

My journey to coaching

For over 12 years, public speaking has been a consistent part of my life. I've delivered talks and presentations across many settings, ranging from small groups to audiences of 2,000+.

My journey didn't begin with confidence. Like many, I had to work through hesitation, overthinking, and uncertainty in front of audiences. Through disciplined practice, repeated experience, and the guidance of strong mentors, I learned how to organize my thoughts and speak with greater clarity and composure.

Today, I help working professionals improve the way they communicate in real-world speaking situations — not through performance tricks or motivational pep talks, but through preparation, structure, and deliberate practice.

Why I Coach This Way

Built for working professionals

I focus specifically on working professionals because the situations that matter most aren't keynote stages — they're the meetings, presentations, and conversations that shape your career week after week. Speaking well in those moments doesn't require a stage persona. It requires preparation, structure, and steady practice.

That's the work I've spent more than a decade doing for myself. And it's the work I now do alongside clients — with a clear plan for each session, real-time feedback, and honest, practical adjustments you can apply right away.

Every session has one goal: leave you better prepared for the next time it counts.
